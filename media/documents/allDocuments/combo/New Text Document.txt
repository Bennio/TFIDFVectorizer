Elon Musk was born on June 28, 1971, in Pretoria, Transvaal, South Africa,[23] the son of Maye Musk (née Haldeman), a model and dietician from Regina, Saskatchewan, Canada,[24] and Errol Musk, a South African electromechanical engineer, pilot, and sailor. Elon Musk has a younger brother, Kimbal (born 1972), and a younger sister, Tosca (born 1974).[28] His paternal grandmother was British, and Elon Musk also has Pennsylvania Dutch ancestry.[29][30] His maternal grandfather was American, from Minnesota.[31] After his parents divorced in 1980, Musk lived mostly with his father in the suburbs of Pretoria,[29] which Musk chose two years after his parents split up, but now says was "not a good idea".[32] As an adult, Musk has severed relations with his father.[32] Elon Musk has a half sister and half brother.[33][34]
